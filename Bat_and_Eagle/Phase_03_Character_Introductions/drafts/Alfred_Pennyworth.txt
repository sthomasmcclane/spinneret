CHARACTER_NAME: Alfred Pennyworth
Layer One: Batman's loyal butler and trusted confidante, offering counsel and unwavering support.

Layer Two: breaking down the introduction, and expanding
Loyal butler - How long has he served the Wayne family? Since Bruce's childhood, possibly even before, having served his parents. What are his duties beyond typical butler tasks? He is Batman's medic, quartermaster, strategist, and emotional support. He manages the Batcave's resources, maintains equipment, and provides logistical support. How does he manage the dual life of Bruce Wayne/Batman? With meticulous planning, discretion, and an unparalleled understanding of Bruce's needs, often anticipating them before Bruce himself.
Trusted confidante - What kind of advice does he give? His advice is a blend of pragmatic wisdom, moral guidance, and emotional support. He often reminds Bruce of his humanity and the importance of his code. How does he handle Batman's darker moments? With gentle firmness, a dry wit, and an unwavering belief in Bruce's inherent goodness, pulling him back from the brink when necessary. What is the nature of their relationship? A deep, paternal bond, far beyond that of employer and employee. Alfred is Bruce's surrogate father and closest family.
Offering counsel - How does he deliver his advice? Often subtly, through rhetorical questions or observations, but can be direct and stern when the situation demands it. Does Batman always listen? Not always immediately, but he always considers Alfred's words, even if he initially resists them.
Unwavering support - How does he show his support? Through his constant presence, his tireless work behind the scenes, his medical care, and his emotional availability. Has his support ever wavered? Never, though he has expressed concern and fear for Bruce's safety and sanity.

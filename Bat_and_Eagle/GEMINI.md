# Style Guidelines
- tone: Gritty, cynical, hard-boiled.
- POV: Third-person limited.
- Tense: Past tense.

# Writing Rules
- Call out the details of Eagle One's costume, equipment, and methods so as to juxtapose them against Batman's own BUT DO NOT MAKE DIRECT COMPARISONS - leave that to the reader.
- Keep sentences punchy. Fragments are okay.
- Dialogue should be sparse and subtext-heavy.


# World Lore
- It is always raining in this city.
- Batman does not know the 'Eagle One' codename until after Oracle has done some very deep investigating so the prose should not use that moniker until after the scene where this happens.

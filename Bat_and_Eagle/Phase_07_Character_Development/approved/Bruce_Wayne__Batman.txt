CHARACTER_NAME: Bruce Wayne / Batman
### Voice
*   **As Bruce Wayne:** Reserved, articulate, often speaks in measured tones, can be charming and diplomatic in public, but privately more direct and burdened. Uses a sophisticated vocabulary.
*   **As Batman:** Gruff, low growl, terse, uses minimal words, speaks with authority and intimidation. Commands attention.

### Characterisation
*   **Drives:** Justice, preventing others from suffering the same trauma he experienced, protecting Gotham, upholding his non-lethal code.
*   **Relates to the world:** Sees Gotham as a broken city he is sworn to protect, views criminals as a symptom of systemic failure, deeply distrusts authority (especially corrupt elements), isolated by his mission.
*   **Archetype:** The Vigilante, The Guardian, The Tragic Hero.
*   **Internal Motivation:** Atonement for his parents' death, a deep-seated need to bring order to chaos, fear of becoming what he fights.
*   **External Motivation:** Stopping crime, dismantling corruption, protecting innocent lives, confronting threats like Eagle One.

### Questionnaire
*   **What is their greatest fear?** Failing Gotham, breaking his non-lethal code, becoming a murderer, losing those he cares about (Alfred, Oracle).
*   **What is their most treasured possession?** The memory of his parents, the Batcave (as a sanctuary and operational hub), his non-lethal code.
*   **What is their biggest regret?** Not being able to save his parents, the collateral damage his war on crime sometimes causes.
*   **How do they handle stress?** Internalizes it, becomes more withdrawn, trains more intensely, seeks counsel from Alfred and Oracle.
*   **What is their secret ambition?** To see a Gotham that no longer needs Batman.
*   **What is their greatest strength?** Unwavering resolve, intellect, physical prowess, moral compass.
*   **What is their greatest weakness?** His isolation, his rigid adherence to his code (can be a disadvantage), his inability to fully trust others.

### History
*   **Childhood:** Traumatic experience of witnessing his parents' murder, leading to a vow to fight crime.
*   **Teenage Years/Early Adulthood:** Extensive global training in martial arts, criminology, forensics, and various scientific disciplines. Developed the Batman persona.
*   **Recent History:** Operating as Batman in a hostile Gotham, constantly battling corruption and crime, maintaining a fragile peace, deeply isolated.

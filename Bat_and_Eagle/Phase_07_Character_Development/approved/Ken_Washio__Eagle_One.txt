CHARACTER_NAME: Ken Washio / Eagle One
### Voice
*   **As Ken Washio (if he has a public persona):** Likely quiet, observant, perhaps a bit detached.
*   **As Eagle One:** Cold, direct, utterly devoid of remorse or hesitation. Speaks with a chilling efficiency, focused solely on the task.

### Characterisation
*   **Drives:** Justice (as he perceives it), dismantling criminal networks, eliminating those he deems involved in corruption/crime.
*   **Relates to the world:** Sees the world in black and white, believes lethal force is necessary for true justice, views Batman's non-lethal approach as weak and ineffective.
*   **Archetype:** The Anti-Hero, The Government-indoctrinated Hunter/Killer.
*   **Internal Motivation:** A deep-seated belief that the ends justify the means, and a complete obedience to his orders.
*   **External Motivation:** Eradicating crime, bringing swift and permanent justice, fullfilling his official (yet clandestine) mandate.

### Questionnaire
*   **What is their greatest fear?** Failure to achieve his objectives, the continued existence of crime and corruption.
*   **What is their most treasured possession?** His bird-themed boomerangs (tools of his trade), his unique "Eagle Style" and "Bird Run" abilities.
*   **What is their biggest regret?** Unlikely to have many, as he operates with conviction, but perhaps a past miscalculation or a moment of perceived weakness.
*   **How do they handle stress?** Becomes more aggressive, doubles down on his lethal tactics, focuses intensely on his mission.
*   **What is their secret ambition?** To cleanse Gotham (or perhaps other cities) of all criminal elements, to prove his country, government, and team are superior.
*   **What is their greatest strength?** Ruthless efficiency, tactical mastery, speed, agility, unwavering conviction.
*   **What is their greatest weakness?** His inflexibility, his inability to compromise, his disregard for collateral damage, his isolation.

### History
*   **Past:**  Ken is the leader and tactician of the Japanese Government's elite Science Ninja Team. His 'Bird Style' is based on the Eagle. When not in costume, Ken works as an aircraft test pilot and delivers parcels by air mail. He lost his father, also a pilot, mysteriously when he was a child and his mother died when he was 11, leaving him an orphan. He started training for his role in the Science Ninja Team at 13.
*   **Motivation for coming to Gotham:** Unclear, but likely dispatched by his handlers as Gotham's pervasive corruption and crime is fertile soil for his agency's arch enemies.

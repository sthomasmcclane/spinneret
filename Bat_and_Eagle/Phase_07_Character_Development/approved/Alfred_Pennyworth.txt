CHARACTER_NAME: Alfred Pennyworth
### Voice
*   Refined, calm, articulate, often uses dry wit and subtle sarcasm, speaks with a paternal warmth and unwavering loyalty.

### Characterisation
*   **Drives:** Protecting Bruce, upholding the Wayne legacy, providing moral guidance, maintaining the Batcave and Wayne Manor.
*   **Relates to the world:** Sees the harsh realities of Gotham but maintains a hopeful outlook for Bruce, acts as Bruce's anchor to humanity.
*   **Archetype:** The Mentor, The Caretaker, The Confidante.
*   **Internal Motivation:** Deep love and devotion to Bruce, a sense of duty to the Wayne family, a desire for Bruce's well-being.
*   **External Motivation:** Supporting Batman's mission, providing practical assistance, offering counsel.

### Questionnaire
*   **What is their greatest fear?** Losing Bruce, Bruce compromising his humanity, Gotham falling completely.
*   **What is their most treasured possession?** The memories of Thomas and Martha Wayne, his relationship with Bruce.
*   **What is their biggest regret?** Perhaps not being able to prevent the tragedy that befell the Waynes, or not being able to convince Bruce to live a "normal" life.
*   **How do they handle stress?** Remains outwardly calm, focuses on practical solutions, offers quiet reassurance, sometimes uses humor to diffuse tension.
*   **What is their secret ambition?** To see Bruce find peace and happiness, even if it means giving up being Batman.
*   **What is their greatest strength?** Unwavering loyalty, wisdom, emotional intelligence, practical skills (medical, engineering, culinary).
*   **What is their greatest weakness?** His deep emotional attachment to Bruce, which can make him vulnerable.

### History
*   **Past:** Former British Secret Service, highly skilled in various fields. Became the Wayne family butler and guardian to young Bruce after his parents' death.
*   **Relationship with Bruce:** Paternal figure, confidante, moral compass. Has been with Bruce through every step of his journey as Batman.

CHARACTER_NAME: Barbara Gordon / Oracle
### Voice
*   Intelligent, sharp, quick-witted, speaks with precision and clarity, often uses technical jargon, can be sarcastic or dry.

### Characterisation
*   **Drives:** Information gathering, strategic analysis, supporting Batman's mission, fighting crime through intelligence.
*   **Relates to the world:** Sees the interconnectedness of information and power, believes knowledge is the ultimate weapon, frustrated by physical limitations but empowered by her intellect.
*   **Archetype:** The Strategist, The Oracle, The Hacker.
*   **Internal Motivation:** A desire to contribute to justice despite her physical limitations, a need to use her intellect to make a difference.
*   **External Motivation:** Providing intelligence to Batman, dismantling criminal networks, exposing corruption.

### Questionnaire
*   **What is their greatest fear?** Losing her mental faculties, her systems being compromised, failing Batman due to a lack of information.
*   **What is their most treasured possession?** Her technological hub, her vast network of information.
*   **What is their biggest regret?** The incident that led to her paralysis (being shot by the Joker).
*   **How do they handle stress?** Becomes more focused, dives deeper into data, uses her intellect to find solutions.
*   **What is their secret ambition?** To create a truly secure and just information network for Gotham, or perhaps to inspire others to fight crime through intelligence.
*   **What is their greatest strength?** Unparalleled intellect, technological prowess, strategic thinking, resilience.
*   **What is their greatest weakness?** Her physical limitations, her reliance on technology, potential for information overload.

### History
*   **Past:** Daughter of Commissioner Gordon, formerly Batgirl. Paralyzed after being shot by the Joker.
*   **Transformation:** Transitioned from Batgirl to Oracle, becoming Batman's primary intelligence gatherer and strategic advisor. Built an extensive information network.

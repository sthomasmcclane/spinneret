SCENE_NAME: Rooftop Clash with Eagle One
Gotham's Rooftops. Night. Batman, guided by Oracle's intelligence, confronts Eagle One atop a towering building. The wind whips their capes. Batman moves first, a shadow striking with calculated precision, aiming to disarm. Eagle One counters with raw, lethal force, his bird-themed boomerangs a blur. The battle rages across the shattered concrete and twisted metal of the rooftops. Batman employs his martial arts to incapacitate, deflecting lethal blows, seeking openings for a non-lethal takedown. Eagle One uses his "Bird Run," scaling walls, moving with impossible agility. He shouts a cryptic phrase in Japanese about "restoring true order." Batman recognizes a specific, highly advanced martial arts move, a technique taught only to elite government operatives in Asia. Despite Batman's strategic brilliance, Eagle One's ruthless efficiency and unexpected tactical advantages allow him to escape into the night. The visible destruction and the continued presence of the lethal vigilante fuel further public outcry and fear.

SCENE_NAME: Eagle One's Violent Arrival
Gotham City. Night. Rain slicks the grimy streets. Batman perches on a gargoyle, observing a tense standoff between two rival gangs in a dimly lit alley. He sees the fear in the citizens' eyes, the pervasive distrust. Suddenly, a blur of motion. Eagle One descends, a dark silhouette against the neon glow. He moves with chilling efficiency, bird-themed boomerangs slicing through the air. Gang members fall, dead. Batman watches, horrified, as Eagle One executes a mid-level gang leader with a precise, lethal strike. The remaining gang members scatter in terror. Public screams echo. News choppers arrive, their spotlights cutting through the rain, capturing the brutal aftermath. Batman clenches his jaw, recognizing the immediate, existential threat to Gotham's fragile peace. He knows this new vigilante's methods will ignite the city.

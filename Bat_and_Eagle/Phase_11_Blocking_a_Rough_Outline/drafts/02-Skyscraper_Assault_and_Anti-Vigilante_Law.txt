SCENE_NAME: Skyscraper Assault and Anti-Vigilante Law
Gotham City. Night. A heavily fortified skyscraper, gleaming with corporate power, is the target of a sophisticated money-laundering operation. Corrupt GCPD officers stand guard, complicit. Eagle One strikes, a whirlwind of lethal force. He breaches security, moving through the building with ruthless efficiency. Bird-themed boomerangs fly, incapacitating and killing syndicate enforcers. Several high-ranking police officers, caught in the crossfire or directly involved, also fall. Chaos erupts. News channels broadcast the devastation live. Public opinion polarizes, some praising Eagle One's decisive action, others condemning the escalating violence. Oracle, in her hidden hub, intercepts encrypted communications between a GCPD captain and a crime syndicate boss, confirming their alliance. She also analyzes a damaged boomerang recovered from the scene, noting its unique Japanese metallurgy. The corrupt city council, seizing on the public's fear, swiftly passes draconian anti-vigilante legislation. Batman, now an outlaw, watches the news reports from a shadowed rooftop, his face grim.

SCENE_NAME: Batcave Introspection and Oracle's Counsel
The Batcave. Night. Batman, weary and isolated, stands before the glowing Batcomputer screens, reviewing footage of Eagle One's latest rampage. Public condemnation weighs heavily. He feels the immense pressure to compromise his non-lethal code. A vivid flashback hits him: during his global training, he almost killed a criminal, only to be pulled back by the memory of his parents' faces. Alfred approaches, a cup of tea in hand. He speaks calmly, referencing a past British Secret Service mission where "necessary evil" led to unforeseen devastation, subtly guiding Bruce away from lethal tactics. Simultaneously, Oracle, from her hidden technological hub, meticulously analyzes Eagle One's fighting style, predicting movements, identifying vulnerabilities. She recalls her own paralysis, the moment her intellect became her greatest weapon, strengthening her resolve to find a non-lethal solution. Batman listens to Alfred's wisdom, processes Oracle's data, and reaffirms his unwavering commitment to his code. He begins to formulate a strategy to neutralize Eagle One without bloodshed.

SCENE_NAME: Skyscraper Catastrophe and Batman's Retreat
Gotham City. Day. Eagle One launches an assault on a heavily fortified skyscraper, aiming to dismantle a major criminal network. Unbeknownst to him, the building also houses several secretly corrupt city officials. Eagle One breaches the building, engaging criminals. Oracle, reviewing surveillance footage, notices a pre-planted device detonates *after* Eagle One has broken off his assault, originating from a specific office. The resulting explosion and structural collapse cause widespread destruction, claiming lives of underworld figures, innocent civilians, and corrupt office-holders. The GCPD immediately blames Batman for the chaos. Widespread riots erupt across Gotham. Looting, arson, and violent clashes become commonplace. Batman, his reputation utterly shattered, is relentlessly hunted by both criminals seeking revenge and authorities. He retreats into the deepest shadows of Gotham, convinced he has failed the city he swore to protect. Ken Washio receives a coded message from his handlers, expressing "disappointment" in the collateral damage but reaffirming his mission. An encrypted data chip, linking corrupt officials to the crime syndicate, is found in the debris and anonymously delivered to Oracle.

SCENE_NAME: Re-emergence and Final Strategy
The Batcave. Night. Batman, having endured his dark night of the soul, emerges from the shadows with renewed resolve. Alfred approaches, sharing a story about Thomas Wayne's unwavering commitment to Gotham, even in the face of overwhelming odds, further solidifying Bruce's purpose. Oracle, from her hidden hub, has meticulously analyzed the incriminating data chip and intercepted communications. She devises a plan to leak the evidence to a trusted, independent journalist, ensuring it reaches the public without suppression. Batman, now with a clearer purpose, meticulously plans a final, climactic confrontation with Eagle One. His objective is twofold: neutralize the lethal vigilante without further bloodshed and dismantle the corrupt power structure exploiting Gotham's chaos. He prepares for a brutal, extended engagement, a fight for Gotham's very soul.

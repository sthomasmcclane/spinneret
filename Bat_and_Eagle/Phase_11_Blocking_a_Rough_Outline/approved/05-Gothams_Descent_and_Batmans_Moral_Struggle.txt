SCENE_NAME: Gotham's Descent and Batman's Moral Struggle
The Batcave. Night. Gotham spirals further into chaos. News reports show the corrupt GCPD actively spreading disinformation, blaming Batman for the city's collapse and subtly praising Eagle One's "decisive" actions. Batman watches, his face etched with internal pressure. He grapples with agonizing questions: is his non-lethal code still viable? Is a more aggressive approach necessary? The faces of his murdered parents haunt him from a nearby photograph, a constant reminder of his vow. He paces the cavernous space, the weight of Gotham's fate pressing down. He considers the blurring line between hero and villain. After hours of intense introspection, a dark night of the soul, Batman reaffirms his unwavering commitment to his non-lethal code. His resolve hardens, his purpose becomes clearer. He will not break his vow.

SCENE_NAME: Climactic Showdown and Gotham's Healing
Gotham's Rooftops. Night. Batman engages Eagle One in a brutal, extended confrontation. Eagle One fights with the ferocity of a cornered animal, his lethal boomerangs a constant threat. Batman, pushing his limits, seeks to disarm and incapacitate without lethal force. He uses his knowledge of Eagle One's elite training, creating an opening. He incapacitates Eagle One. Simultaneously, Oracle's meticulously gathered evidence, including the incriminating data chip and intercepted communications, is leaked to the public. The revelations send shockwaves through Gotham. Corrupt city council members and GCPD accomplices are exposed, leading to arrests and the dismantling of their power. The immediate threat of city-wide war is averted. Public order slowly restores. Japanese government agents swiftly arrive, removing Ken Washio from Gotham custody. Batman returns to the shadows, a silent guardian, his presence a subtle reassurance. Gotham, scarred but resilient, begins the long process of healing and rebuilding.

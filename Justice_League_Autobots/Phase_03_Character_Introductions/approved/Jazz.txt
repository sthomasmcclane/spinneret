CHARACTER_NAME: Jazz
### Layer One: The gameshow introduction.
The Autobots' stylish special-ops lieutenant who is obsessed with human culture and fighting with "pizzazz."

### Layer Two: breaking down the introduction, and expanding.
**Stylish:** How does it manifest? He chooses the flashiest vehicle modes (Porsche 911) and uses a high-wattage sound system to blast music during combat. He moves with a rhythmic, breakdance-inspired fighting style.
**Special-ops lieutenant:** Why is he so capable? Behind the "cool" exterior is one of Optimus's most trusted soldiers. He is an expert in sabotage and urban infiltration. He can "blend in" to any culture effortlessly.
**Obsessed with human culture:** How does this show? He uses human slang, watches human movies, and is the first Autobot to truly "get" the concept of a superhero "brand."
**Pizzazz:** How does it affect him? He is the bridge between the Autobots and the human public. His partnership with Booster Gold is a match made in heaven; they both understand that a hero needs to be "seen" to be effective.

### Layer Three: Basic Info and story role.
*   **Designation:** Jazz
*   **Rank:** Special Operations / Lieutenant
*   **What motivates this character?** Protecting the diversity and "soul" of life on Earth while looking good doing it.
*   **Single sentence summary of character's role in story:** Jazz acts as the "Face" of the Autobots, working with Booster Gold to maintain public morale and provide kinetic, high-speed support.
*   **Single paragraph summary of the character's role in story:** Jazz is the ultimate "Infiltrator-Liaison." He uses his sonic emitters to disrupt Decepticon communications while Booster Gold broadcasts their victories to the world. He provides the "soul" to the mechanical side of the alliance, often being the one to cheer up the younger heroes like Cyborg or Booster. His role is to keep the Decepticons off-balance with unpredictable, high-energy tactics that defy Prowl's rigid logic, making him the "chaos" element on the side of the heroes.

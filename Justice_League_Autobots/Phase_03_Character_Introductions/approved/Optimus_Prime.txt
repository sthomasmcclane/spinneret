CHARACTER_NAME: Optimus Prime
### Layer One: The gameshow introduction.
A wise and compassionate leader of the Autobots, bearing the weight of his people's history and a deep hope for peace.

### Layer Two: breaking down the introduction, and expanding.
**Wise:** How does he show this? Through his measured speech, his ability to see the bigger picture beyond immediate conflicts, and his deep understanding of long-term consequences derived from millennia of experience. He's learned invaluable lessons from centuries of devastating conflict.
**Compassionate leader:** How does he lead? He leads by example, inspiring unwavering loyalty and trust in his troops. He consistently prioritizes the safety and well-being of others, always seeking a peaceful resolution first before resorting to force. He possesses a profound empathy for all sentient life.
**Of the Autobots:** What does this mean? He is a sentient, transforming robotic being from the planet Cybertron, leader of the heroic faction in a civil war against the Decepticons. His people are refugees in search of a new home.
**Bearing the weight of his people's history:** What history? The devastating civil war that destroyed Cybertron, rendered it uninhabitable, and forced his people into exile across the galaxy. How does it affect him? He carries a deep sense of guilt and responsibility for the fate of his world and his species, constantly striving to avoid repeating past mistakes and always wary of conflicts escalating beyond control.
**Deep hope for peace:** Despite all the loss, suffering, and seemingly endless war he has endured, he still clings to the belief in the possibility of lasting peace and harmonious coexistence between different species, even with their former enemies. This unwavering hope drives his actions and his desire to protect nascent life.

### Layer Three: Basic Info and story role.
*   **Full Name:** Optimus Prime
*   **Age:** Millennia old (in Cybertronian terms, making him ancient and experienced)
*   **What motivates this character?** The protection of all sentient life, the tireless pursuit of peace, and ensuring the survival and honorable future of his people while guiding other races towards a better, more united future.
*   **Single sentence summary of character's role in story:** Optimus Prime serves as the primary mentor figure, educating the Justice League on Cybertronian threats and guiding their critical alliance.
*   **Single paragraph summary of the character's role in story:** Optimus Prime's dramatic arrival marks a crucial turning point for the Justice League, providing invaluable, first-hand knowledge about the true nature of Megatron and the wider Cybertronian threat. He quickly forms a bond of respect with the Justice League, particularly Batman, acting as a wise, experienced, and morally upright mentor. He shares his people's tragic history, elucidates the dangers of uncontrolled Cybertronian technology, and emphasizes the profound ethical considerations of dealing with such immense power. His steadfast moral compass and strategic insights are vital in shaping the alliance, helping the heroes understand not just *how* to fight Megatron, but *why* their unity, cooperation, and adherence to their principles are absolutely essential for victory.

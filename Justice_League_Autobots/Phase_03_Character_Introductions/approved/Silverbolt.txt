CHARACTER_NAME: Silverbolt
### Layer One: The gameshow introduction.
A noble, chivalrous aerialbot who serves as the "Knight of the Sky," despite a secret, paralyzing fear of heights.

### Layer Two: breaking down the introduction, and expanding.
**Noble and Chivalrous:** How does it manifest? He speaks with an old-world formality, addressing Wonder Woman as "Your Highness" or "Princess." He believes in a warrior’s code of honor and will never strike an unarmed foe.
**Knight of the Sky:** Why does he take this role? He was built to lead an aerial squadron. He views himself as a sentinel watching over the world from the clouds. 
**Fear of heights:** How does this show? When he's not in jet mode, he stays close to the ground and is prone to vertigo. He only flies because his sense of duty outweighs his personal terror.
**F-35 Stealth Fighter:** How does it affect him? His vehicle mode is the ultimate "Invisible Plane" homage. He provides Wonder Woman with a cloaked, Mach-3 platform, allowing her to strike with the speed of a thunderbolt.

### Layer Three: Basic Info and story role.
*   **Designation:** Silverbolt
*   **Rank:** Aerialbot Leader
*   **What motivates this character?** Living up to the standards of heroism he sees in Wonder Woman and overcoming his own internal fears.
*   **Single sentence summary of character's role in story:** Silverbolt serves as Wonder Woman’s primary transport and aerial combat partner, managing the sky-front of the war.
*   **Single paragraph summary of the character's role in story:** Silverbolt is the "Aerial Vanguard." He and Diana are the first into any combat zone, dropping from the stratosphere under a cloak of invisibility. His role is to engage the Seekers (Starscream’s unit) while Diana board’s the enemy carriers. Throughout the story, he finds his courage by watching Diana’s fearless nature, eventually leading the charge against the Decepticon orbital platform in the final act.

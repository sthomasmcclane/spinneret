CHARACTER_NAME: Wonder Woman
### Layer One: The gameshow introduction.
An ancient Amazonian warrior-princess and diplomat who combines divine strength with a relentless pursuit of truth and peace.

### Layer Two: breaking down the introduction, and expanding.
**Ancient Warrior:** How does it manifest? She has thousands of years of combat training. She is aggressive and decisive in battle, possessing a "warrior's spirit" that can sometimes seem cold or intimidating to humans. She views combat as a sacred duty to protect the weak.
**Warrior-Princess:** Why does she operate this way? Raised on Themyscira, she was born to lead and protect. She carries herself with a royal grace and expects honor from both allies and enemies. She has little patience for cowardice or deceit.
**Relentless pursuit of truth:** How does this show? Through her Lasso of Hestia and her blunt, honest manner of speaking. She values clarity above all else and is often the first to see through Decepticon or LexCorp misinformation.
**Divine Strength:** How does it affect her? As a demigod, she is one of the few who can go toe-to-toe with a Cybertronian in robot mode. Her partnership with Silverbolt reflects her affinity for "Knights"—she treats him as an equal soldier in a divine crusade rather than a mere vehicle.

### Layer Three: Basic Info and story role.
*   **Full Name:** Diana of Themyscira
*   **Age:** Chronologically centuries old; appears in her 30s.
*   **What motivates this character?** Her mission to bring the "World of Men" into a state of peace and her personal code of honor.
*   **Single sentence summary of character's role in story:** Wonder Woman acts as the League’s frontline general, using Silverbolt to provide global Mach-3 strike capabilities.
*   **Single paragraph summary of the character's role in story:** Diana is the first to recognize the Transformers as "spirits" of metal, treating them with the respect she would show a Greek god. She leads the aerial assault against the Seekers, using Silverbolt’s stealth capabilities to bypass Decepticon scanners. Her role is to keep the battle focused and honorable, often acting as a bridge for Silverbolt, who struggles with his own internal fears. Her unwavering courage is the catalyst that allows the Autobots to fully commit to the defense of a world that isn't their own.

CHARACTER_NAME: Superman
### Layer One: The gameshow introduction.
The "Man of Steel" and a beacon of hope who strives to lead by example while balancing god-like power with human humility.

### Layer Two: breaking down the introduction, and expanding.
**Beacon of Hope:** How does it manifest? He is the public face of the Justice League, often the first to arrive and the last to leave. His presence inspires calm in civilians and courage in his allies. He speaks with a measured, compassionate tone and avoids lethal force whenever possible.
**Human Humility:** Why does he maintain this? Raised as Clark Kent in Smallville, his core values are rooted in Midwestern modesty. He views himself as a guest on Earth who must earn the trust of its people every day, rather than a ruler who demands it.
**God-like Power:** How does this show? He possesses near-invulnerability, flight, heat vision, and super strength. However, this power creates a constant fear of "collateral damage." He is always holding back, a "man of cardboard" in a world of glass, which causes him immense internal stress during high-stakes battles.
**Balancing Power and Humility:** How does it affect him? He struggles with the loneliness of his heritage. While he is the "Man of Tomorrow," he is often haunted by the loss of Krypton. This makes him gravitate toward Optimus Prime, finding a kindred spirit in another displaced leader who carries the weight of an entire species.

### Layer Three: Basic Info and story role.
*   **Full Name:** Kal-El / Clark Kent
*   **Age:** Late 30s
*   **What motivates this character?** A deep-seated belief in the inherent goodness of people and a promise to his adoptive parents to use his gifts for the benefit of humanity.
*   **Single sentence summary of character's role in story:** Superman serves as the alliance’s moral North Star, providing the raw power needed to halt Decepticon advances while learning the nuances of planetary defense from Optimus Prime.
*   **Single paragraph summary of the character's role in story:** Superman provides the essential heavy-lifting and aerial superiority for the League, but he finds himself out of his depth regarding the "total war" philosophy of the Decepticons. His partnership with Optimus Prime is transformative; through Optimus, Clark learns to accept the burden of leadership on a galactic scale. He acts as the ultimate deterrent against Megatron's Fusion Cannon, often putting his own body on the line to shield the city, and serves as the primary diplomat ensuring that the world's governments don't turn on their new mechanical allies.

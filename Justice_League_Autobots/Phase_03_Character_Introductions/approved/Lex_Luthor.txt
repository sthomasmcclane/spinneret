CHARACTER_NAME: Lex Luthor
### Layer One: The gameshow introduction.
A ruthless, brilliant industrialist obsessed with human superiority and controlling Earth's destiny.

### Layer Two: breaking down the introduction, and expanding.
**Ruthless:** How does this manifest? He will stop at nothing to achieve his goals, even if it means exploiting others, endangering innocent lives, or undermining global security and established governments. He possesses no moral qualms or ethical boundaries when pursuing power and dominance, seeing all actions as justified by his perceived ultimate goal.
**Brilliant industrialist:** What industries? LexCorp is a sprawling conglomerate, a titan in fields such as technology, advanced science, defense, and urban development. He is a genius inventor, scientist, and shrewd businessman, capable of groundbreaking (and often dangerous) discoveries and corporate maneuvering.
**Obsessed with human superiority:** Why? He believes humanity is inherently superior to all other species and should not rely on "alien" saviors like Superman or the newly arrived Autobots. He perceives all extraterrestrial beings, regardless of their intentions, as an existential threat to humanity's autonomy, potential, and future self-determination.
**Controlling Earth's destiny:** What does this mean? He harbors an unwavering desire to dictate the direction of human advancement, technological progress, and global governance. He genuinely believes himself to be the only individual truly capable of leading humanity to its full potential, free from external influence or perceived alien interference.

### Layer Three: Basic Info and story role.
*   **Full Name:** Alexander "Lex" Luthor
*   **Age:** Late 40s to Early 50s
*   **What motivates this character?** His unwavering belief in human supremacy, his insatiable desire for ultimate power and control, and his deep-seated hatred and intense jealousy of Superman and all other extraterrestrial beings he views as threats to his vision of humanity.
*   **Single sentence summary of character's role in story:** Lex Luthor is the secondary antagonist whose covert reverse-engineering of Cybertronian tech inadvertently enabled the crisis and serves as a tempting, dangerous "ally."
*   **Single paragraph summary of the character's role in story:** Lex Luthor is subtly revealed as the dangerous, clandestine architect behind the initial Cybertronian "discovery," having secretly reverse-engineered alien technology for LexCorp's gain. His profound hubris in believing he could control such immense power leads directly to Megatron falling into the Joker's maniacal hands, unleashing the global crisis. During the ensuing chaos, he shrewdly positions himself as a potential "savior," offering LexCorp's tainted assistance and advanced weaponry to the desperate Justice League, tempting them with morally compromising and ethically questionable solutions. He serves as a critical subplot, highlighting the dangers of unchecked human ambition and the misuse of advanced technology, ensuring that even after the Joker is defeated, the lingering threat of alien tech falling into the wrong human hands remains.

In a modern DC Universe overseen by the Justice League, humanity thrives, unaware of LexCorp's clandestine reverse-engineering of alien "Cybertronian" technology. Simultaneously, a small group of Autobots secretly observes Earth, assessing its worthiness for a potential alliance.

The fragile peace shatters when the Joker, through cunning chaos, acquires LexCorp's ultimate weapon: the sentient Cybertronian Fusion Cannon, Megatron. Wielding this unimaginable power, he plunges major cities into unprecedented destruction. The Justice League is mobilized but overwhelmed by Megatron's alien capabilities. Witnessing the heroes' struggle and recognizing a familiar Cybertronian threat, the Autobots reveal themselves. They dramatically enter the battle, forming urgent, strategic partnerships with specific Justice League members—Optimus Prime with Superman, Batman with Prowl, Cyborg with Ratchet, Wonder Woman with Silverbolt, Aquaman with Depthcharge, Hal Jordan with Cosmos, Booster Gold with Jazz, and Catwoman with the Arcee Triplets—offering both critical power and newfound legitimacy.

Forced into an uneasy alliance, the Justice League and Autobots quickly realize Megatron is more than a weapon; it's a sentient entity subtly influencing the Joker's already deranged mind. The threat escalates from a supervillain rampage to an alien invasion, compelling the combined heroes to commit to a global pursuit to prevent the planet's irreversible descent into anarchy.

Optimus Prime and Ratchet become crucial mentors, educating Batman and Cyborg on Cybertronian history, Megatron's destructive sentience, and its vulnerabilities. Batman, aided by Prowl's logical processing, develops advanced strategies, while Cyborg, guided by Ratchet, begins to understand the alien technology. Their initial, coordinated confrontation with Joker/Megatron in a major city proves devastating; a landmark is destroyed, heroes are severely injured, and they fail to contain the threat, revealing glimpses of LexCorp's prior Earth-based modifications to the weapon.

In the wake of this defeat, Lex Luthor shrewdly offers LexCorp's "assistance," tempting the desperate heroes with potentially tainted advanced weapons and intel, cleverly obscuring his own role in creating the crisis. The Joker, now nearly symbiotically linked with Megatron, unleashes a catastrophic assault on a global capital, bringing it to its knees. The combined forces are decimated, hope seems lost, and the alliance strains to its breaking point amidst public despair.

Rallying from their darkest hour, the Justice League and Autobots launch a desperate, all-or-nothing final assault. Utilizing their combined knowledge, a new strategy crafted by Batman and Prowl, and Cyborg's reverse-engineered countermeasures developed with Ratchet, they confront the Joker/Megatron. The brutal clash focuses on neutralizing the Joker's madness, counteracting Megatron's power, and severing their symbiotic link, exploiting weaknesses from LexCorp's original tampering.

The Joker is defeated, and Megatron is finally neutralized, its destructive will contained. The world is saved, albeit scarred. The Justice League and Autobots solidify their alliance, becoming permanent partners in Earth's defense. Lex Luthor escapes full accountability, but LexCorp's dangerous R&D and the true presence of Cybertronians on Earth are exposed to the world, ushering in a new era where humanity understands that future alien threats will necessitate enduring, interspecies alliances.

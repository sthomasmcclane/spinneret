### **Synopsis: Justice League Autobots**

**Core Premise**
In a world where LexCorp has secretly reverse-engineered alien "Cybertronian" technology for decades, the Joker awakens and steals the most powerful artifact in existence: **Megatron**, a sentient, mass-shifting Fusion Cannon. Sensing Megatron’s activation, a group of hidden Autobots emerge from the shadows. To prevent global panic and combat the Decepticon-backed villain alliance, the Autobots seek out the Justice League to serve as their emissaries and partners.

**The Setting**
*   **Earth (DC Universe):** A modern world where Lex Luthor has monopolized "Project NBE" (Non-Biological Extraterrestrial) tech to fuel his global empire.
*   **The Conflict:** The Joker (wielding Megatron) and Lex Luthor (backed by Soundwave and the Dreads) vs. the Justice League and their Autobot Titan Units.

**The Partnerships (Titan Units)**
*   **Batman & Prowl:** Prowl infiltrates the Batcave as a **Rezvani ‘Dark Knight’ (Chassis 101/100)** to prove his strategic value. They function as a tactical-logic unit.
*   **Superman & Optimus Prime:** Optimus approaches Clark as a **Western Star Semi-Truck**. They serve as the moral leaders of the alliance, with Optimus's trailer acting as a mobile civilian triage center.
*   **Wonder Woman & Silverbolt:** Silverbolt is an **F-35 Lightning II Stealth Fighter** who uses mimetic cloaking. He views Diana as a warrior-queen and provides Mach-3 global deployment.
*   **Aquaman & Depthcharge:** A **Cybertronian Manta-Class Submersible**. They are maritime hunters who track Decepticon signatures in the deep ocean via magnetic distortions.
*   **Green Lantern (Hal Jordan) & Cosmos:** A **Silver Orbital Scout (UFO)**. Cosmos acts as Hal’s "eye in the sky" and personal surveillance system while Hal is off-world.
*   **Catwoman & The Arcee Triplets:** Three **Ducati Panigale Superbikes** sharing a hive-mind. They partner for high-stakes infiltration and urban stealth.

**Key Plot Points**
1.  **The Theft:** Joker triggers a security breach at LexCorp Sub-Level 9, stealing the "Core Weapon" (Megatron).
2.  **The Approach:** The Autobots individually reveal themselves to JLA members, offering advanced technology and partnership in exchange for the League "vouching" for their presence on Earth.
3.  **The Alliance:** The Justice League adopts the Autobots as their primary tactical vehicles, integrating alien hardware with superheroic abilities.
4.  **The Threat:** The Joker uses Megatron’s Fusion Cannon mode to level city blocks, while Lex Luthor attempts to seize control of the "Spark" technology to build a Decepticon army.

Act 1

Introduction to the protagonist's world
Eye-scream and Soft Serve exist on the fringes of mutant society, their ice cream-based powers making them objects of ridicule and disgust rather than awe. Eye-scream, with his ability to project freezing beams and manipulate ice, is constantly melting and reforming, a grotesque figure of instability. Soft Serve, whose body is composed of malleable, edible ice cream, struggles with maintaining her form and is often seen as a novelty. Their shared experience of ostracization, particularly from the X-Men who represent the "acceptable" face of mutantkind, fosters a deep-seated resentment and a bizarre, almost symbiotic bond. They find solace and understanding only in each other, their complementary powers and shared bitterness drawing them into a dark, intimate relationship.

Call to action
The catalyst for their revenge comes after a particularly humiliating encounter with a junior X-Men team, who easily subdue them during a minor incident, treating them more as a nuisance than a threat. This public dismissal ignites their simmering rage. Eye-scream, in a moment of chilling clarity, proposes the ultimate act of infiltration and vengeance: Soft Serve will consume him entirely, transforming into a living, edible Trojan Horse. This plan, born from their unique powers and twisted intimacy, becomes their shared obsession, a way to prove their worth and inflict pain on those who scorned them. The fetishistic nature of their bond deepens as they commit to this extreme act, blurring the lines between consumption, intimacy, and destruction.

Crossing the threshold
The act of consumption itself serves as their irreversible crossing of the threshold. In a secluded, desolate location, Eye-scream willingly allows Soft Serve to engulf him, his icy form dissolving into her creamy mass. This is a profound physical and psychological transformation for both. Eye-scream experiences a terrifying loss of self, becoming one with Soft Serve's being, while Soft Serve grapples with the sensation of containing another consciousness within her. This act severs their ties to their previous, individual existences, binding them together in a singular, monstrous entity. There is no turning back; they are now a unified weapon, committed to their dark purpose, their individual identities subsumed by their shared mission of revenge.

Act 2

Mentor teaches the lead
Their "mentor" is the very act of their fusion and the unique, unsettling synergy it creates. As Eye-scream resides within Soft Serve, they discover new ways to communicate and coordinate their powers. Eye-scream learns to subtly influence Soft Serve's form and temperature from within, guiding her movements and enhancing her abilities. Soft Serve, in turn, learns to channel Eye-scream's icy projections through her own body, creating unexpected and devastating attacks. This internal, almost telepathic connection, born from their extreme proximity, refines their combined abilities, teaching them to operate as a single, formidable entity, far more dangerous than they were individually. Their shared consciousness becomes their guide, pushing them to explore the full, terrifying potential of their merged state.

First challenge
Their infiltration into the X-Men's headquarters is surprisingly smooth, Soft Serve's innocuous appearance allowing her to bypass initial security. Their first challenge arises when they encounter a low-level X-Men trainee in a deserted corridor. Eye-scream, from within, urges Soft Serve to test their new capabilities. Soft Serve subtly manipulates the environment, creating a localized ice storm that traps the trainee without revealing her true nature. This small victory boosts their confidence, confirming the efficacy of their "Trojan Horse" strategy and the terrifying potential of their combined powers. They revel in the X-Men's ignorance, savoring the anticipation of the chaos they are about to unleash.

Temptation
A moment of temptation arises when Soft Serve, observing the camaraderie and purpose within the X-Men's ranks, experiences a flicker of doubt. The warmth and acceptance she witnesses are a stark contrast to her own outcast existence, and a part of her yearns for such belonging. Eye-scream, sensing her wavering resolve from within, unleashes a surge of his icy bitterness, reminding her of their shared humiliation and the X-Men's hypocrisy. He tempts her with the promise of ultimate power and the sweet taste of revenge, reinforcing their dark bond and extinguishing her momentary weakness. The allure of their destructive path proves stronger than the fleeting desire for acceptance.

Dark moment
The X-Men's telepath, sensing a faint, unsettling psychic residue within the mansion, raises an alarm. Security is heightened, and the X-Men begin a systematic sweep. Eye-scream and Soft Serve are forced to hide, their combined form pressed against cold walls, their powers barely contained. They are nearly discovered when a powerful mutant's energy signature passes dangerously close. In this moment of extreme peril, Eye-scream, from within, feels a surge of despair, questioning the feasibility of their plan. Soft Serve, overwhelmed by the pressure and the proximity of their enemies, nearly loses control of her form, threatening to expose them. They are pushed to their absolute limits, teetering on the brink of failure, their dark bond strained but ultimately holding.

Act 3

Final conflict
Their cover is finally blown when a curious X-Man investigates a lingering cold spot and discovers the unusual composition of Soft Serve. The X-Men converge, realizing the true nature of the threat. The final conflict erupts in a spectacular and bizarre battle within the X-Men's headquarters. Eye-scream fully manifests from within Soft Serve, creating a terrifying, ice-cream-infused monstrosity. They unleash a barrage of freezing attacks, manipulating the environment with their combined powers, turning the mansion into a frozen battlefield. The X-Men struggle to combat their unique abilities, as Eye-scream and Soft Serve fight with a desperate, unified fury, fueled by years of resentment and their twisted bond, aiming to dismantle the very symbol of their oppression.

Return home
The aftermath of the battle leaves the X-Men's headquarters in disarray, a testament to the bizarre and destructive power of Eye-scream and Soft Serve. Whether they achieve their ultimate revenge or are ultimately defeated, their "return home" is not to a place, but to a new, altered state of being. If victorious, they retreat, their bond solidified by their shared triumph, forever bound by the act of consumption and destruction. If defeated, they are captured, their unique physiology posing a new challenge for the X-Men, but their dark, fetishistic bond remains, a testament to their unwavering commitment to each other and their shared hatred. Regardless of the outcome, they are irrevocably changed, their individual identities forever intertwined by their monstrous journey.
